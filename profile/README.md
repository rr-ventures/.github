# rr-ventures

I build products and internal tools that solve real workflow problems.

What matters to me most is taking something messy, manual, or frustrating and turning it into software that gives people time back, reduces guesswork, and makes the next step clearer. Most of the work here starts with a genuine use case, not a trend.

## Selected Products

### [MixingSongFinder](https://github.com/rr-ventures/me-favouritesongsfinder-app)
Built for the problem of music discovery feeling shallow and repetitive. It helps turn scattered sources, curators, and recommendations into a better way to find tracks that are actually worth keeping.

### [Lightroom Preset Selector](https://github.com/rr-ventures/me-tinderphotomaxxing-app)
Built for the problem of having too many photos and not knowing which ones are strongest, what edits they need, or how to improve them efficiently. It helps people move from a messy photo set to a smaller set of better final images.

### [Playlist Saver](https://github.com/rr-ventures/product-playlistsaver-app)
Built for anyone who has lost tracks from Spotify or YouTube playlists without warning. It gives people a record of what changed and alerts them when songs disappear, so playlists stop feeling fragile.

### [AI Content Coach](https://github.com/rr-ventures/product-performingcontentfinder-app)
Built for creators and businesses who are tired of guessing why content performs. It turns post history, competitor activity, and video transcripts into something you can actually learn from and act on.

### [AU Property Ops Copilot](https://github.com/rr-ventures/product-aiproptech-app)
Built for property work that usually lives across notes, spreadsheets, mental checklists, and scattered tools. It brings analysis, due diligence, feasibility, and renovation planning into one clearer operating flow.

### [MediaDL](https://github.com/rr-ventures/me-mediaDL-app)
Built for the everyday friction of moving between download tools, converters, transcription tools, and media utilities. It brings those jobs into one place so the workflow feels simpler and more usable.

## Other Active Apps

## Philosophy

I like building things that earn their place by being useful.

That usually means products that remove manual effort, reduce uncertainty, improve visibility, or make a process feel much more manageable. I am interested in AI for the same reason: not as decoration, but as a way to make software more helpful in practice.

## Stack

Mostly working across React, Next.js, Python, FastAPI, Node.js, TypeScript, PostgreSQL, SQLite, Tailwind, Streamlit, Electron, and AI tooling around Gemini, Claude, Ollama, and workflow automation.
